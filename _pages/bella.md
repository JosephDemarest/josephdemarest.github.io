Bella exudes a serene grace, her black and white fur and quietly commanding presence telling the story of an old stray who has been both mother and survivor. Her eyes, deep and knowing, hint at the wisdom gained from years of caring for her kittens, including Peanut and Nugget, with a subtlety that mirrors a dancer's light footfall. She's the archetype of a nurturing spirit, her distance not a barrier but a respectful nod to independence, even as she remains intimately connected with those she cherishes. Bella's affection is given selectively, making it all the more precious—a silent testament to a life spent in the ballet of survival and tenderness.
